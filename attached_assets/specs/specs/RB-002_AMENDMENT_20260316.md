# RB-002 Amendment Record

**Date:** 16 March 2026

---

During the RB-003 requirements session, a foundational rule regarding the admission of invoices to the invoice graph was clarified. For conceptual correctness, this rule has been formally documented as an amendment to the RB-002 artefact set.

## New Artefact

*   **ADR-020: Invoice Graph Admission Rule** has been created and added to the RB-002 series. This ADR formally states that an invoice's admission to the active graph is gated by the creditor's `verification_status`, not the debtor's.

## Impact

This amendment does not change any existing RB-002 artefacts but adds a new, critical constraint that was previously implicit. It provides a more complete and robust definition of the invoice graph's integrity rules.
